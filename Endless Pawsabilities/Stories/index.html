Max

From Rags To Riches

Max is the most American dog you can get. Born into an impoverished family in Canda, Max was able to cross the border into America after being captured by dog capturers. He lived a difficult life, but always kept up his indominable spirit. Eventually, an owner decided to adaopt Max. Fun, loving, and an insatiable appetite for fun and games, Max truly is the quinsisential part of American Dogs.

Max also has had a loving owner. His favorite snacks are chicken, and a treats. His previous owners allowed Max to hunt, but recently, he has not caught any live prey. Maybe it is due to his new owners kindess and love. Max still loves to hang out with his family and enjoys things. It will be interesting to see what becomes of Max in the near future. Max was also one of the first pets donated for Endless Pawsabilities.

Kitty

The Shabby Tabby

Kitty is a beautiful young tabby cat that has tried her best to be able to be the best kind of kitty there is. However, fate has not been kind to her. She has tried her hardest to fit in, but has also been trying to figure out different emotional values. Thanks to Endless Pawsabiliteis, she has been able to find ways of making things work for her. Kitty is in a much better place with her new owners.

Kittys favorite food is tuna. She loves playing outside, and tries to do different things. Simply put, she cannot sit still too long in her own house. Her previous owners forbade her from leaving her home. Her new owners are the opposite, for Kitty is allowed to run around and do things she could have only dreampy of with her previous owners.
